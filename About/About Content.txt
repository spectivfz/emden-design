Interior design, fashion design, and product branding
Richard Emden | Founder
A luxury fashion and interiors designer with more than 20 years of professional experience, Richard Emden is a master of creative visualisation. His demonstrated expertise has seen him work with a diverse array of global names, leading design projects from ideation through to execution via his eponymous brand, Emden Design.

Emden Design began as a luxury fashion brand in late-90s London, before expanding into Asia with original clothing and accessories lines that would retail in more than 200 luxury stores worldwide, including six standalone Emden outlets. Today, Richard has shifted creative focus to branch into the interior design realm, with projects that include luxury holiday villas in Phuket and luxury home interiors across Thailand.

Over the years, Richard has developed a rich network of elite craftspeople and premium wholesalers across Asia. He works closely with suppliers and factories to hone the manufacturing process for greater efficiency and optimised revenue for clients. This established infrastructure of trusted design, manufacturing and construction teams continues to help bring Emden Design visions to life

At the core of Emden Design is a philosophy that defies the rule that simplicity comes at the expense of identity. Richard brings individualistic expression to every project, ensuring each product or space is a luxurious blend of artistry, vibrancy, fine materials and exquisite craftsmanship.
