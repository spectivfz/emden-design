Richard Emden · Founder
The Studio

Two decades of creative visualisation.

A luxury fashion and interiors designer with more than twenty years of professional experience, Richard Emden is a master of creative visualisation — leading projects from ideation through to execution for a diverse array of global names.

Emden Design began as a luxury fashion house in late-90s London before expanding into Asia, with original lines retailing in over 200 luxury stores worldwide and six standalone Emden outlets. Today the focus turns to interiors — luxury holiday villas in Phuket and refined homes across Thailand — supported by a rich network of elite craftspeople and premium wholesalers.
